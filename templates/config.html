GPS HAT not detected. Check that the LC29H(BS) HAT is seated on the GPIO header and the UART jumper is in Position B. Survey-In is unavailable until the GPS HAT is connected.

MowerBase averages its own GPS for the survey duration and locks to that position. Absolute accuracy is ~1–3 m but relative accuracy (base to mower) is centimetre-level.

NTRIP Client

Home WiFi required for NTRIP relay. Go to Network to connect to your home WiFi. You can save credentials below but NTRIP relay will not work until WiFi is connected.

MowerBase connects to an external NTRIP caster and relays its corrections to the mower via SiK radio and the local NTRIP server simultaneously.
